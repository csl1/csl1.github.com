About Colette Leung

My name is Colette Leung. You can follow me on Twitter (librarylettie).

I am currently a graduate student at the University of Alberta, working on a joint degree in Humanities Computing and Library and Information Science. My thesis concerns the use of digital mapping technology, and digital timelines, to create visual representations of provenance metadata for rare books and manuscripts, allowing scholars to visualize and analyse information rapidly.
